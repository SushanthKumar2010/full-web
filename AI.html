Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Learn how machines are getting smarter and changing our world.

🤖 What is Artificial Intelligence?


AI refers to machines or software that mimic human intelligence. This includes learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and even creativity!



📌 Why It’s a Big Deal


      Powers everything from chatbots to self-driving cars
      Used in healthcare, finance, education, entertainment, and more
      It’s shaping the future of jobs and industries


🧠 Key Concepts in AI


      Machine Learning: AI that learns from data
      Neural Networks: Algorithms inspired by the human brain
      Natural Language Processing: Teaching machines to understand human language
      Computer Vision: Enabling machines to “see” and interpret images


👦 Why Teens Should Learn AI


AI is not just for scientists—teens can build smart projects, explore ethical tech, and prepare for tomorrow’s careers with AI skills.
