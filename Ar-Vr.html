🧠 What is AR/VR?


AR (Augmented Reality) overlays digital content on the real world, while VR (Virtual Reality) creates immersive experiences in a fully virtual environment. Together, they’re transforming industries like gaming, education, healthcare, and retail.



🔧 Skills You’ll Learn


      AR/VR development using Unity and Unreal Engine
      3D modeling and interaction design
      Mobile AR using ARKit and ARCore
      XR (Extended Reality) design principles
      Storytelling for immersive tech


🎮 Why It’s Exciting


AR/VR is not just for gaming—it's shaping the future of learning, marketing, simulation, and even social interaction. As a teen creator, you can design next-gen experiences from your laptop.
