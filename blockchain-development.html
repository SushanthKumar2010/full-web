🔗 What is Blockchain?


Blockchain is a decentralized and secure digital ledger that records transactions across multiple computers. It powers cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and smart contracts—creating trust without the need for intermediaries.



🎯 Why It’s Important


      Secure and transparent data sharing
      Disrupts traditional finance, healthcare, and more
      Supports decentralized apps (Web3)
      Empowers creators, developers, and future entrepreneurs


💡 What You'll Learn in This Course


      Basics of blockchain and how it works
      What are blocks, nodes, and consensus mechanisms
      How Bitcoin and Ethereum operate
      Real-world applications of blockchain


🧠 Skills You’ll Gain


      Understanding of decentralized systems
      Knowledge of cryptocurrency fundamentals
      Smart contract logic (foundation for dApps)
      Tech vocabulary used in Web3 and crypto spaces


👦 Why Teens Should Explore This


Blockchain is the foundation of future tech. Teens who learn this early can build apps, invest smartly, and even launch Web3 startups.
