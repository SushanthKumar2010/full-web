📊 CA Rachana Ranade

Empowering India with simplified financial education

👩‍🏫 About Rachana Ranade


CA Rachana Ranade is a Chartered Accountant, educator, and entrepreneur from Pune, India. She is renowned for her ability to demystify complex financial concepts, making them accessible to a broad audience. Through her engaging content, she has become a leading voice in financial literacy.



🎓 Educational Background


      BCom in Statistics from Brihan Maharashtra College of Commerce (2002–2007)
      Chartered Accountant from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (2008)
      PG Diploma in Business Management (2009–2011)
      Master's Degree in Business Studies from Savitribai Phule Pune University (2011–2012)


🚀 Career Highlights


      Over 10 years of experience teaching auditing to CA and MBA students
      Launched her YouTube channel in 2019, amassing over 4.6 million subscribers
      Developed 7 comprehensive courses on stock market and personal finance
      Initiated the 'Har Ghar Investor' campaign to promote financial literacy across India


📹 YouTube Channel


Rachana's YouTube channel offers a wealth of knowledge on stock markets, investment strategies, and financial planning. Her clear and concise explanations have helped millions understand and navigate the financial world.
