🌐 What is Google Digital Garage?


Google Digital Garage is a free online learning platform from Google, offering courses in digital marketing, career development, data, tech, and more. It’s designed to help individuals — especially students and small business owners — build digital skills for modern careers.



📚 What Can You Learn?


      Digital Marketing Basics – Learn SEO, SEM, and social media strategy
      Data & Tech – Intro to coding, machine learning, and productivity tools
      Career Development – Interview prep, resume writing, confidence-building


🎓 Certification


Many courses come with a free certificate from Google — perfect to boost your LinkedIn or resume.



👦 Why It’s Great for Teens


Teengro recommends Google Digital Garage because it's beginner-friendly, 100% free, and teaches in-demand skills teens can use in freelancing, internships, and future careers.
