🧠 What is AI & ML?


Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to simulate human intelligence. Machine Learning (ML) is a branch of AI that teaches computers to learn from data and make predictions or decisions.



🎯 Why Learn AI as a Teen?


      Understand how tools like ChatGPT and Alexa work
      Build smart apps and bots
      Enter a booming tech field with massive career potential
      Learn how data powers everything from Netflix recommendations to self-driving cars


📚 Topics You’ll Explore on Khan Academy


      Neural networks and how they mimic the brain
      Supervised vs unsupervised learning
      Ethics of AI and bias in algorithms
      Real-world applications like AI in medicine, art, and language


🚀 Skills You’ll Gain


      Logical and critical thinking
      Basic programming in Python (used in many AI tools)
      Understanding datasets and pattern recognition
      Awareness of technology’s impact on society
