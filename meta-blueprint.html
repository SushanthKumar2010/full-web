📘 What is Meta Blueprint?


Meta Blueprint is Meta's official learning platform that offers free online courses and certifications to help people and businesses grow using Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger.



🎯 Why Learn from Meta?


      Learn directly from the platform creators
      Get certified with globally recognized credentials
      Understand ad strategies, targeting, and business tools

📚 What You Can Learn


      Facebook Ads: Learn how to create, run, and optimize ad campaigns.
      Instagram Strategy: Learn how to build your brand and engage users.
      WhatsApp for Business: Use automation and messaging tools to support customers.
      Measurement & Insights: Learn how to track ad performance and audience behavior.

Teens interested in digital marketing, branding, freelancing, or business can use Meta Blueprint to gain in-demand skills and even get certified.
