Teachable Machine

Teach your computer new tricks with machine learning—no coding needed!

🤖 What is Teachable Machine?


Teachable Machine is a web-based tool from Google that lets you train machine learning models with your own data. It’s easy to use and doesn’t require any coding skills—just upload your own images, sounds, or poses, and watch the machine learn!



🎯 Why It’s Awesome


      Train your own AI models using simple images, sounds, and poses
      Fun, interactive way to learn about machine learning
      Build projects like classifiers, gesture-based games, or even interactive art


🧠 How Does It Work?


      Choose Your Data: Upload images, sounds, or poses to teach the machine.
      Train the Model: The AI learns from your data and builds a model.
      Test It: Use your new AI model to make predictions in real time.


👦 Why Teens Should Try It


Teachable Machine makes it easy to dive into machine learning without getting overwhelmed by complex code. It’s perfect for teens who want to experiment with AI, understand the basics of machine learning, and create their own projects.
